Accomodation Assistance

We understand the importance of providing comprehensive support to our students throughout their time abroad. One such support service is free accommodation assistance. At Bonne Chance , we understand that finding suitable accommodation is one of the biggest challenges facing students studying abroad. To help with this, we offer a free accommodation assistance service to all of our students. Our goal is to ensure that our students have a safe, comfortable and affordable place to call home during their time abroad. To start, our team provides students with information on the local housing market, including options such as homestays, student residences and apartments.
